GAME RULES

Control the snake to eat apples and grow. Use the arrow keys to change the snake's direction. With each eaten apple, the snake grows larger. The game ends when the snake eats itself. Be careful, if the snake passes through the other side of the wall, not to bite its tail.

Earn one point for each eaten apple. To restart the game, press the restart button.

Choose the board you want to play on.
